Thoughts Become Things

Hi, I'm Alex, a Mechatronics Engineer with a passion for turning ideas into reality. With expertise in manufacturing and project management, I help bring your concepts to life through innovative design, prototyping, and production.

When I'm not engineering solutions, you'll find me creating music, experimenting with concrete art, crafting jewelry, or exploring new technologies. I believe the best innovations come from combining technical expertise with creative thinking.

Whether it's developing custom software, designing manufacturing solutions, or helping you navigate the entire product development process, I approach every project with the same curiosity and attention to detail that drives my personal creative pursuits.

Work

Product Development & Manufacturing

From concept to production, I guide you through the complete product development cycle. This includes 3D CAD design, prototyping, testing, material selection, and full-scale manufacturing. I manage the entire process from initial design through final delivery, including logistics and nearshore manufacturing coordination between Mexico and the United States. Additionally, I design and build custom fixtures, jigs, and specialized tools including automation needs to streamline assembly processes and improve workplace safety.

Technology Solutions & Digital Strategy

I develop custom software solutions and applications tailored to your business needs, using modern development and AI tools to create efficient, scalable software. My services include comprehensive tech support and helping businesses establish and maintain their online presence through custom website development and strategic digital positioning, with a specialized team handling social media management and comprehensive online strategy to enhance your market presence.
